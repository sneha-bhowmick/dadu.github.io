The bond between a grandparent and a grandchild is a uniquely special one. Unlike our parents, who have the tough job of balancing both love and discipline, kindness and firmness, our grandparents are able to be never-ending founts of love. This especially holds true for grandfathers, who are able to bask in the joys of grandparenthood in ways they may not have as parents themselves.By the very nature of the relationship, we have less time than we'd like with our grandfathers, and our days and years are all the sweeter for it.
